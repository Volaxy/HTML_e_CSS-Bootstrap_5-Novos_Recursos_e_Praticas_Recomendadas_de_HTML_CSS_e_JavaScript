Traditional Coffees

Serenatto coffee is known for its traditional and tasty blends, which appeal to drink lovers. Our beans are carefully selected and roasted to produce a rich aroma and balanced flavor.

Among the house's most traditional coffees, "Café Serenatto" stands out, an exclusive blend of beans with notes of chocolate and caramel. Another popular option is the "Café Italiano", a full-bodied and intense espresso. "Café Cappuccino" is a classic choice for those who prefer a creamy and smooth drink.

Special Coffees

We are also known for our specialty coffees, which are carefully prepared with selected high-quality beans and precise preparation techniques..

Among the specialty coffee options at Café & Bistrô Serenatto, we highlight aeropress coffee, which is prepared using an immersion and pressure technique, resulting in a drink with an intense flavor and accentuated aroma. Another popular option is Hario V60 coffee, which is prepared with a cone-shaped paper filter and results in a clean and smooth drink, highlighting the sensorial notes of the beans. Additionally, we offer a variety of cold drinks, such as iced coffee, which is prepared with dark roasted beans and served with ice and milk, resulting in a refreshing and tasty drink.
